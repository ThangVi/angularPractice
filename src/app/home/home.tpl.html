edmonds racing

Come join us at Edmonds Racing for an experience in the dynamic world of Thoroughbred Horse Racing!

Based on the Gold Coast at Bundall, where we believe the climate and facilities, will enable your horses to produce their best performance possible on raceday.
The mix of old school horsemanship, taught to him by two greats in Neville Begg and Peter Snowden, and new training techniques learnt by vying his trade in some of the best training centres in Australia, has given Toby the necessary tools to compete with the best.

Want to Race a horse with us?

Toby believes in his ability to place his horses to perfection, giving owners the ultimate chance at success that we strive for. Toby offers first class facilities at the Gold Coast Turf Club, home to the famous Magic Millions Carnival, and also the time honoured Hollindale Stakes.

After spending 2010 being Patinack Farms Queensland foreman, he is now training back in his own right and ready to firmly entrench himself as one of Queenslands leading trainers. Toby's facilities consist of a 37 horse barn, 8 horse walker, a beautiful canal walk to the track where the horses can stop for a pick of grass and all the world class training tracks. With nearby beaches frequently utilized there is every reason to believe your horse will be a winner with Edmonds Racing!

Come and experience the thrill of racehorse ownership in the Sunshine State with Edmonds Racing, making horse owners dreams of success, become a reality!
